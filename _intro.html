Preface

The information contained within is strictly confidential. If you are reading this and are not a government official or a licensed medical practioner then please return this book to the St. Denis Doctors Office, St. Denis, LE at your earliest convenience.

This is a collection of notes I have taken from observations, books, sketches I have made, and photos I have taken or acquired while doing my research in The New Territories.

I have found many new plants I have not encountered while living in Éire. Any and all plants I encounter in the New Territories will be catalogued in this book.

With the use of office pharmaceutical/apothecary equipment and apparatus I have produced several medicines using locally sourced plants, chemicals and materials. This will benefit the New Territories as we will not have to outsource or order from elsewhere.

All information is correct at the time of publishing. Any usage of misinformation gained from the pages within this book are at your own discretion.
For rectifications please speak to Head Pharmacist Einín Springs, Pharmacie T.N.T., St. Denis, LE. Erroneus information will be amended upon the next publication.

 

Signed:
Dr. Einín Springs, 1900
