Bewitching Balm

Balm

Treats head trauma (it will also keep you from falling under the spell of a witch - or take you on a journey inside your head)

Add 8 cups of water to a cold cauldron or pot. Add 2 cups of violet snowdrop and 10 drops of opium or morphine. Stir well.
Add 1 part wax, 1 part oil, 1 tablespoon of salt to the mixture.
Leave in a warm room stirring frequently (every hour or so). Alternatively put over a very low heat.
Once the wax has dissolved and the only solids are the snowdrops - strain and put in to vessels.
Leave in a cool place until the mixture hardens, then secure a lid.

Apply a small amount to the top of the neck. APPLYING TOO MUCH WILL CAUSE YOU TO GO ON A SPIRITUAL JOURNEY. ONLY DO SO UNDER GUIDANCE.

External Uses

  • Apply to the upper neck for head trauma.
  • Protects you from witches spells and charms.
  • If you wish to go on a spiritual journey, apply generously at the top of your neck and relax.
