Cough/throat Syrup

Syrup

Very strong anti inflammatory. Also a strong cold and cough relief.

Slowly simmer 4 cups of fresh Alaskan Ginseng in a pot of water for half an hour.
Remove solids, then add 6 cups of sugar and boil off half of the liquid. Add 2 jars of honey once the liquid is halved.

Continue to boil until syrup, cool then bottle.
MUST BE USED WITHIN 2 WEEKS
