Blueberry Tea

Brew

Bring 3 ½ cups of water to a boil in a pot or cauldron. Add a handful of blueberries. Lower to a steady simmer for 20 minutes.

Take from the heat. Chop roughly the root of 1 Yarrow and the leaves of 1 American Ginseng. Add to the brew and leave to steep for 15 minutes.

Strain in to a pint jar, add 4 tablepoons of honey and stir well.

Cover and drink as needed. Drink no more than 2 pints a day. TOO MUCH WILL CAUSE NAUSEA AND POSSIBLY DIARRHEA

Internal Uses

  • Will flush the body of common toxins.
  • Relieves fever and colds.
  • Helps with low energy.
  • Mild relief from menstrual cramps.
