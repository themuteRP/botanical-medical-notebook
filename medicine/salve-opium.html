Opium Salve

Salve

Water in a cold cauldron or pot. Add opium or morphine at required potency.

Add salve soap (1 part beeswax, 1 part lard, 1 part mint oil, 1 table spoon sodium chloride).

Leave in warm room open to the air. Mixing frequently until most of the water has evaporated. Mix every hour or couple of hours as required until all is left is the paste.

Use sparingly as it can be addictive. NOT TO BE INGESTED

External Uses

  • Apply to areas of extreme pain or swelling.
  • Apply a small amount to gauze or bandage to slow the effects and for lesser area application.
  • Similar effect to morphine but for external use.
