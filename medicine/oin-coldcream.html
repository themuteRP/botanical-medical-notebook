Cold Cream

Ointment

Water in a large cold cauldron or pot. Add the leaves of 5 Hummingbird Sage plants and 1-3 drops of arsenic depending on size of vessel. Stir well.

Reduces chance of wounds going septic. DO NOT APPLY DIRECTLY TO SKIN. DO NOT INGEST ORALLY.

External Uses

  • Apply a small amount to gauze or bandage to reduce or stop wounds going septic.
