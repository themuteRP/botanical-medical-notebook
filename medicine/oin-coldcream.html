Cold Cream

Ointment

Squeeze the juice of one lime in to a pot or cauldron. Coarsely chop the root of one Yarrow and the remainder of the Lime.

Add to lime juice with a cup of water and simmer for 60 minutes. Strain the Yarrow and Lime solids and place back on a gentle heat.

Add 6 oz wax, 6 oz fat, 1 pint mint oil. Simmer until most of the water has gone and remove from heat.

Pour in to containers then allow to cool ready for distribution.

Protects chapped skin and can alleviate fever. Use sparingly. NOT FOR USE AROUND THE EYES. SAFE TO CONSUME HOWEVER NOT RECOMMENDED.

External Uses

  • Apply to chapped lips, sore noses or dry skin from cold and damp weather.
