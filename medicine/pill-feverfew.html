Feverfew Tablets

Pills

Grind 2 bunch of dried Wild Feverfew, 1 bunch of dried Alaskan Ginseng, 4 ounces limestone into a paste.

Roll the paste in to 2 sausage shapes, put upon a pill machine and cut. Makes approx 20.

Take no more than 2 at a time. DO NOT TAKE MORE THAN 3 EVERY 24 HOURS

Internal Uses

  • Swallow with water. Relieves mild pain. 2 if the patient faces harsh pain.
