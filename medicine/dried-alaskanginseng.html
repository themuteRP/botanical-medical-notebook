Dried Alaskan Ginseng Stem

Herb or dry mix

Hang dry Alaskan Ginseng until dried.

Leave in warm dry room to the open air.

Induces vomiting if chewed dry.

Burn and use the ashes with tonics or brews to soothe the stomach.

Internal Uses

  • Chew the dried stem until vomiting occurs. Rehydrate once stomach contents have been expunged.
  • Burn the stem and crush resulting ash. Can be added to tonics to soothe stomach complaints.
