The New Territories Diseases,

their symptoms and medicines for relief

Common Cold

Cough Syrup / Herbal Mix

Symptoms

  • Runny nose
  • Sore throat
  • Cough
  • Congestion
  • Slight body aches or a mild headache
  • Sneezing

Cholera

Raspberry Syrup / Gut Tonic

Symptoms

  • Profuse watery diarrhea, sometimes described as "rice-water stools"
  • Vomiting
  • Thirst
  • Leg cramps
  • Restlessness or irritability

Malaria

Feverfew Syrup / Lessfever Brew

Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Headache
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Abdominal pain
  • Muscle or joint pain
  • Fatigue
  • Cough

Typhus

Ginseng Tablets / Blueberry Tea

Symptoms

  • Headache
  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Rash

Immersion Foot

Sage Bandage / Opium Salve

Symptoms

  • Blisters
  • Blotchy skin
  • Redness
  • Skin tissue that dies and falls off
  • Coldness
  • Heaviness
  • Numbness
  • Pain when exposed to heat
  • Persistent itching
  • Prickliness
  • Tingling

Conjunctivitis

Blueberry Serum / Sage Eye Rinse

Symptoms

  • Redness in one or both eyes
  • Itchiness in one or both eyes
  • A gritty feeling in one or both eyes
  • A discharge in one or both eyes
  • Tearing
