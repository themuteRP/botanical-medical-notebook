Gut Tonic

Tonic

Relief from nausea, stomach pains/cramps and gas.

Add 1 cup of English Mace to 8 cups of water in a pot.
Bring to the boil, then simmer for 30 minutes.
Add the bark or pulp of a willow. Cover and simmer for another 30 minutes.
Strain then add the honey and alcohol, mix well. Bottle ready for distribution.

The tonic can be added to a meal if preferred.

Internal Uses

  • Consume the tonic. Either from the bottle or add to food for slower relief.
