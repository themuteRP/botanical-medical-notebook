English Mace:

Achillea Ageratum

Growth
The perennials have a clumpforming habit and reach heights of 50 to 60 centimetres.

Leaves
Achillea ageratum has silvery grey, simple leaves that are alternate. The leaves are linear and pinnatifid.

Flowers and Fruits
Achillea ageratum produces light greenish yellow many-stellate flowers.
The perennials produce achenes.

Distribution
Achillea ageratum is native to France and the Iberian Peninsula.
