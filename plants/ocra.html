Ocra

Abelmoschus esculentus

Growth
The plants reach heights of 90 to 150 centimetres.

Leaves
Abelmoschus esculentus is deciduous. The simple leaves are alternate. They are ovate, ciliate and petiolate.

Flowers and Fruits
Abelmoschus esculentus produces solitary light greenish yellow five-stellate flowers in June and July.
The plants carry ornamental loculicidal capsules.

Distribution
Abelmoschus esculentus is native to India and Northeast Africa.
