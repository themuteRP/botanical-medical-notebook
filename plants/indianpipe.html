Indian Pipe:

Monotropa Uniflora

Growth
The perennials reach heights of 5 to 30 centimetres.

Flowers and Fruits
Monotropa uniflora produces solitary white tubular flowers from June to September.
The perennials produce loculicidal capsules.

Distribution
Monotropa uniflora is native to East Asia, North America, Central America and Colombia.

Cultivation
Location and general conditions
The perennials prefer a sunny to shady situation on moist soil.
