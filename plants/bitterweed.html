Bitterweed

Hymenoxys odorata

Growth
The plants reach heights of 10 to 80 centimetres.

Leaves
Hymenoxys odorata has imparipinnate leaves that are alternate. The linear leaflets are entire and petiolate.

Flowers and Fruits
Hymenoxys odorata produces panicles of brilliant greenish yellow many-stellate flowers from April to June.
The plants produce achenes.

Distribution
Hymenoxys odorata is native to California, the Southwest of the US, Colorado, Kansas, the southern Prairie States of the US and Mexico.
