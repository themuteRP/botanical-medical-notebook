Yarrow

Achillea Millefolium

Growth
The perennials have a prostrate growth and reach heights of 20 to 120 centimetres. The plants usually grow to a width of 60 centimetres to 1 metre.

Leaves
Achillea millefolium is semi-deciduous. The middle-green, simple leaves are alternate. They are lanceolate with pinnatisect margins and pinnate venation. They turn an attractive yellow in autumn. The leaves have a glabrous to tomentose surface.

Flowers and Fruits
Achillea millefolium produces corymbs of fragrant erect, white many-stellate flowers from May to July.
The perennials produce brown achenes in summer.

Root system
The plants form rhizomes.

Distribution
Achillea millefolium is native to all of Europe, Turkey, the Caucasus, Iran, the Himalaya, West Sibiria, Central Asia, Australia and New Zealand.

Cultivation
Location and general conditions
The perennials prefer a sunny situation on dry soil. They prefer loamy soil or sandy loam with a pH between 6 and 8. The plants need a soil depth of at least 20 centimetres for good growth. The plants tolerate temperatures down to -35Â°C.

Maintenance
The plants need little to no maintenance if grown under suitable conditions..
Remove runners if no spreading is desired.
Cut back all shoots after flowering considerably to promote healthy growth.

Propagation
Propagate by sowing, by cuttings or by division.

Pests and diseases

  • A powdery white coat on the plants indicates an infection with powdery mildew. Remove affected plants and apply a fungicide. To prevent infection improve ventilation, keep the roots moist and do not water the plants from above.
  • White tufts or white covering on the lower surface of the leaves indicates an infection with downy mildew. Remove affected plants and apply a fungicide. To prevent infection improve ventilation, keep the roots moist and do not water the plants from above.
  • Brown, orange or yellowish pustules on shoots and on the leaves lower surfaces are very likely caused by a fungal infestation (rust). Remove affected parts and apply fungicide. Also improve ventilation and reduce humidity.
