Sugarcane:

Saccharum Officinarum

Growth
The comparatively fast-growing grasses reach heights of 3 to 4 metres, they have a semi-erect habit. The main growing season is in spring and summer.

Leaves
Saccharum officinarum is evergreen. The blue-green, simple leaves are alternate. They are lanceolate with entire margins and parallel venation. The foliage is dense.

Flowers and Fruits
Saccharum officinarum produces panicles of white flowers in January and February.
The grasses produce ornamental brown caryopses in spring.
